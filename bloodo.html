About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of volunteers committed to raising awareness about blood health, including blood groups and blood levels. Our mission is to educate and empower communities with the knowledge they need to lead healthier lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide communities with valuable information about the importance of blood types, blood levels, and their impact on overall health. We aim to distribute this information to various areas and assist people in making informed decisions about their health through free blood tests and medicine distribution.

Our Journey

Founded in 2024, our initiative started with a simple goal: to raise awareness about the importance of blood health. In the past year, we’ve conducted numerous surveys, blood tests, and medicine distribution campaigns. We’ve reached over 1,000 people, educating them about the vital role blood health plays in their well-being.
