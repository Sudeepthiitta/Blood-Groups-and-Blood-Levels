Health Insights

Blood Group Blood Percentage Health Insights Impact on Health
Blood Group A 40% Higher levels of cortisol; may have a higher risk of stress-related health issues. Balanced diet and stress management can help improve health outcomes.
Blood Group B 15% Strong immune system but may be prone to autoimmune disorders. Regular checkups and a balanced lifestyle are essential.
Blood Group O 30% Lower risk of heart disease but higher risk of ulcers and thyroid disorders. High-protein diets and regular exercise are beneficial.
Blood Group AB 15% Mixed characteristics of A and B; may have higher cholesterol levels. Focus on a heart-healthy diet and regular monitoring.
Blood Group A+ 5% May be prone to high blood pressure and inflammation due to cortisol levels. Low-sodium diet and anti-inflammatory foods can help reduce risks.
Blood Group B+ 7% May have a higher susceptibility to diabetes due to insulin resistance. Maintaining a balanced diet with low sugar and regular exercise can mitigate risk.
Blood Group O+ 10% Lower risk of diabetes and heart disease but higher chances of peptic ulcers. Avoiding spicy foods and high-stress environments can help prevent ulcers.
Blood Group AB+ 3% May have a mixed risk for both heart disease and stroke due to cholesterol levels. Maintaining a healthy weight and monitoring cholesterol levels are crucial.
Blood Group A- 1% Higher risk of autoimmune diseases due to genetics. Regular health checkups and managing stress can help reduce the risk.
Blood Group B- 1% Higher immunity but may face challenges in blood clotting and anemia. Iron-rich foods and regular checkups are essential to prevent anemia.
Blood Group O- 2% Can be more vulnerable to infections and other blood-related issues. Vaccinations and regular exercise are crucial to boost immunity.
Blood Group AB- 1% May face challenges with fertility and hormonal imbalance. Regular checkups and maintaining hormonal balance can improve outcomes.
